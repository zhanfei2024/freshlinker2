Recruitment Consultant / Recruiter /Researcher

We’re looking for attentive recruiters with an entrepreneurial mentality to join our team and help grow FreshLinker.

  • What is the role?

    At FreshLinker, recruiters push the boundaries of what’s possible in helping our clients to reach their goals. They are not constrained by the past, choosing to embrace new technologies and methods, instead of laboring over inefficient, old fashioned methods.

    The primary responsibility of this role is simple: help our clients to recruit the possible candidate they can have. To do this, recruiters engage with our clients to explore their recruiting needs through constant communication and translate them into a talent pool for our clients to choose from.

    We’re looking for recruiters that are excellent communicators, results-driven and extremely attentive.

  • What skills do you need?

    At FreshLinker, our clients come from a diverse range of industries. So it’s fine if you come from a recruiting background of a specific niche, in fact, we encourage it. You should be an excellent communicator and you’ll collaborate with your team on how your recruiting methods can be improved using FreshLinker’s technologies. You will also be expected to provide meaningful feedback on the FreshLinker platform and how it can help recruiters and employers to enhance their recruiting efforts.

  • What kind of people are hired in this role?

    We want recruiters who think strategically and execute quickly; passionate people who are driven by their performance.

    You’ll be working with and supported by a team of recruiters and engineers. Your teammates on the recruiting team will include consultants who previously have a strong track record.

Editors / Content Contributors

We’re looking for content contributors who have something to share about today’s job seeking/recruiting environment.

  • What is the role?

    Career Content is a big thing here at FreshLinker. We believe the significant mismatch between employers and job seekers is due to imperfect information. If only young talent job seekers are more informed about their career paths, they wouldn't be so lost when it comes to their turn to apply for jobs and they wouldn't apply for jobs recklessly - which in turns save employers a lot of screening time.

    The primary responsibility of this role is simple: create thought-provoking and engaging career content that entices our young talent users and/or our employer users.

    We’re looking for content creators who have something to share about the job seeking & recruiting environment and are brilliant writers.

  • What skills do you need?

    You should have authentic insights about the job seeking and/or recruiting environment and would love to share them to a wide audience. You will be expected to contribute content on a regular basis and collaborate with recruiters, employers and industry leaders, in order to generate authentic, beautiful career content.

  • What kind of people are hired in this role?

    We want content creators who are not afraid to speak their minds and have a constant thirst for knowledge.
